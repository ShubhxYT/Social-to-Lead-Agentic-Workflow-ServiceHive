# AutoStream Knowledge Base

## About AutoStream
AutoStream is a SaaS platform that provides automated video editing tools for content creators. Our AI-powered tools help you produce professional videos in minutes.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I switch plans?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

### Is there a free trial?
We offer a 7-day free trial on the Pro plan. No credit card required.

### What video formats are supported?
AutoStream supports MP4, MOV, AVI, and MKV input formats. All exports are delivered in MP4.

### Can I use AutoStream for short-form content?
Absolutely! AutoStream works for YouTube videos, Instagram Reels, TikTok clips, and any short-form content.

## Company Policies

### Refund Policy
- Refunds are available within 7 days of purchase.
- No refunds after 7 days from the billing date.
- Unused credits do not roll over to the next month on the Basic plan.

### Support Policy
- 24/7 live support is available exclusively for Pro plan subscribers.
- Basic plan users receive email support with a 48-hour response SLA.
- Both plans include access to our documentation and community forum.

### Data & Privacy
- All uploaded videos are processed securely and deleted after 30 days.
- AutoStream does not use your content to train AI models without explicit consent.
- GDPR and CCPA compliant.
